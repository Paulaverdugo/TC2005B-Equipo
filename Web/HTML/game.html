How To Play:

You can control your character using the WASD keys.
You are able to go in all four directions.

How To Attack:

To attack, aim by using your mouse, and with a click, a projectile will shoot in the direction of the mouse position, until it hits a wall or an enemy.
***Some gadgets will require player input, so they will be assigned in order a number key.

Careful!

When a player is seen by a camera or a guard, the rest of the guards will be notified and they will go towards you!

Each player has different gadgets, get to know each one of them.



Characters:

Cybergladiator:
Has more HP and does more damage.


Gadgets:

  • Cyber Rush: A dash ability.
  • Plasma Shield: A shield that blocks all damage briefly.
  • Overcharge: Briefly doubles all damage.

Codebreaker:
Can hack (disable for 5 seconds) nearby enemies.


Gadgets:

  • Shadow Veil: A cloak that when seen hacks the enemy that saw you.
  • Circuit Breaker: An EMP surge that hacks enemies inside a range for 5 seconds.
  • Phantom Signal: A fake decoy that sends an alarm with the wrong coordinates.

Ghostwalker:
Can go invisible for 5 seconds.

Gadgets:

  • Ghost Vision: The player can see vision cones.
  • Phantom Step: Dash when stealthing.
  • Ghost Blade: One-hit takedown while stealthing. One use only.
